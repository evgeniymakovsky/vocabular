VOCABULAR - the best web-application to learn foreign language words. It based on random-repeat technology of learning, that improve your words learning in new level.

In User Account you can add or remove your words in english and russian language.

Start training page will start your training to learn words, that you added in your User Account page, with random-repeat technology of learning. Push the button "English" and you will have 30 seconds to enter responsive word in russian language. If this word will match with russian word, that you entered in your "User account" - you will take score +1 to this word. Also, you can get promt if you will push respective button.

Statistics page views your words, counts of repeats and scores of words.

In Settings page you can change your password.
